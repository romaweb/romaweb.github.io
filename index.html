The Problem

Exercising on your own is dull.

People are more likely to exercise – and enjoy it – when doing it with, or against, someone they know. However, existing fitness tracking apps do not cater for this, instead focusing on the individual and on data-heavy platforms.

Also, no fitness app is exploiting the boom in influencer/ celebrity/ athlete-led fitness activity – nor the recent trend in home fitness solutions and competitive socialising – to inspire the global community of ordinary people to exercise.

The Solution

GoJoe allows people to:

Exercise in teams with and against friends and celebrities.

Instead of focusing on individual, ‘diary’-type entries and force-feeding data down people’s throats, GoJoe will apply proven ‘social network’ type usability – harnessing celebrity, community and people’s natural tendency to be competitive – to inspire people to get fitter by competing with people they know.
